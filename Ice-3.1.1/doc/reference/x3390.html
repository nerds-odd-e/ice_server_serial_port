Ice::MarshalException

Overview

local exception MarshalException
    extends 
	ProtocolException

This exception is a specialization of ProtocolException that is raised upon an error during marshaling or unmarshaling data.

Derived Exceptions

EncapsulationException, IllegalIndirectionException, MemoryLimitException, NegativeSizeException, NoObjectFactoryException, ProxyUnmarshalException, StringConversionException, UnmarshalOutOfBoundsException
